Where I'm Going

The Road Less Traveled

I'm in uncharted territory.

Every day I see and learn something new.

It's so rewarding to have thrown myself into the trials of this coding adventure. It took a shift in perspective to see the potentially magical applications of coding. My experiences with Unit 1 of my Front-End Techdegree program have left me enthusiastic and excited to learn more.

For so long there was this invisible fence dividing the worlds of art and creativity from science and technology. I'm now seeing how much of a false dichotomy that always was.

I'm approaching web development with a completely fresh perspective. I'm eager to find my unique footing within the larger world of coding, but I'm also aware of how much I have yet to learn.

For the first time though this sense of being in over my head fills me with a sense of adventure rather than anxiety.
